Welcome to Learn with CAN!

This interactive platform will help you learn how to use can and can’t in English through lessons, videos, and quizzes.

📘 Learn the Theory

CAN is used to express ability or permission:

  • I can play the guitar.
  • She can speak English.

CAN’T (cannot) is used to express inability or prohibition:

  • I can’t fly.
  • He can’t drive a car.
